THE PRISM GRID

ARKOM built the Prism Grid to hold fractured space together.

Fifteen sectors maintained its geometry, each carrying part of a vast stabilizing pattern. For a time, the Grid held.

Then the Block-A-Zoids invaded…

Self-replicating geometric machines falling into the Grid, breaking its alignments, and forcing entire sectors into Gridlock.

They don’t need to be fast. They just need you to be slow.

The Orb is the Grid’s final command core. Guided by you, it gathers scattered Prism Crystals, reconnects damaged pathways, and restores each sector before the swarm gets there first.

During Gridlock, lost Prism Relics may briefly emerge — fragments of the Grid’s original memory. Recovering them is optional. Surviving is not.

Cosmic Zaps are emergency discharges, powerful enough to erase every active Block-A-Zoid on the board. Scarce enough that you’ll want to save them for the moment you’re sure you need them. You won’t be sure.

Restore all fifteen sectors and the Grid begins again — under greater pressure. Each cycle brings the system closer to collapse.
